Welcome to Hooked on Cycling Holidays and our cycling holidays throughout Spain .
Our biking vacation in Spain are in four main regions , one region in Northern Spain around Santander and Picos de Europa mountains and the nearby coastline , another in wonderful Andalusia ( Andalucia ) at the southern most tip of Europe , the third in Catalonia ( Catalunya ) and the forth in the Balearic Island of Majorca ( Mallorca ). Our cycling holidays in Spain are ideal for all abilities .
Catalonia , Northern Spain and Majorca being ideal for first time cycle tourists and also experienced cyclists but our tour in Andalusia is only ideal for experienced cyclists .
Cycling Holiday on Majorca ( Mallorca ) A 7 night van support cycle tour in Majorca .
This cycling holiday in Majorca follows the coast and inland to provide you a lovely unique Majorcan experience .
